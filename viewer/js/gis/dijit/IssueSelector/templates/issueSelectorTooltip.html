To view a SLR scenario, please choose one of each of the following:
1 - First choose a source Agency (either USACE or NOAA) for projections
  • USACE = U.S. Army Corp of Engineers sea level projections
  • NOAA = National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration sea level projections
2 – Next choose the projection curve,
C = curve, the higher the number the larger the SLR
USACE has three projections:
  • C1 = USACE Low (Same as NOAA Low)
  • C2 = USACE Intermediate (Same as NOAA Int Low)
  • C4 = USACE High
NOAA has four projections:
  • C1 = NOAA Low (Same as NOAA Low)
  • C2 = NOAA Intermediate Low (Same as NOAA Int)
  • C3 = NOAA Intermediate High
  • C5 = NOAA High
3 - Choose a time period
  • Currently four time periods – 2040, 2060, 2080, 2100
Now click “Show Scenario”
